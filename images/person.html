Our Company



We add life to a lifetime of learning.

We’re the world’s leading learning company, dedicated to helping everyone realize the life they imagine.
Our purpose is to add life
to a lifetime of learning.

At Pearson, our purpose is simple; to add life to a lifetime of learning. We believe that every learning opportunity is a chance for a personal breakthrough. That's why our c.20,000 Pearson employees are committed to creating vibrant and enriching learning experiences designed for real-life impact. We are the world's leading learning company, serving customers in nearly 200 countries with digital content, assessments, qualifications, and data. For us, learning isn't just what we do. It's who we are.
Find more about us ---->

Our vision to help everyone to realize the life they imagine through learning.

We use our knowledge, experience and technology to deliver the world’s best learning products to more people around the world than ever before. We do it because we are here to help people achieve their potential because we know that when we demonstrate the value of learning, we demonstrate the value of our business and transform lives. Find more about what we do --->

We have an integrated business to support customers through their learning journey

Our digital-first business model puts our customers and consumers at the heart of everything we do, enabling us to deliver the best products and learning outcomes to more people than ever before. It drives our success and enables us to grow with – and adapt to – a rapidly changing learning market.
